Discover what to do in Farmerville LA!

Louisiana is known for it's beautiful plantations. In Farmerville you can visit the Edgewood Plantation and stay there as a B&B or rent it for events such as weddings.

Foster Farms Farmerville Plant provides many jobs to those who live in Farmerville and the surronding towns. Give the plant a call to get a free tour of how they process their chicken.

Lake D'Arbonne State Park is a staple of Farmerville. It's a very well taken care of Park where you can bring your RV, or go camping for fun. Events are often held at the State Park, such as Dutch Oven Thanksgiving.

The Ol Hickory Cafe has been a part of Farmerville for years. Locals love the buffet and the wonderful meals they provide. Stop by for a meal of your choice and enjoy time spent with family and friends.

Lake D'Arbonne is one of the largest lakes in Northern Lousisana. People from all over come to the lake to enjoy fishing. Every year Farmerville holds a fishing competition that anyone can participate in!

Excalibur is an exciting palce to bring the whole family for some fun. If you're looking for a way to get out of the house or visiting Farmerville, going to Excalibur can be great way to spend your time.

Sevins, pronounced "Say-vans", is the most popular resturant in Farmerville. They are known for their classic and delicious cajun meals. They aren't open everyday, so make sure to check out their facebook for there resturant hours!

Revelations Books and Gifts is a gift shop located right next to Sevins. The shop is owned and run by the wife of the owner of Sevins. Revelations is a unique gift shop with wonderful southern style gifts for any occasion.

Lake D'Arbonne Point is a wonderful vaction spot right on the lake. There are cabins available to rent or you can bring your own RV. If you're looking for a fun summer get away, this is the place for you!
